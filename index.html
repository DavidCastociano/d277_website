Welcome to New Jersey!

Welcome to the Garden State! Despite its small size, NJ is packed with culture, history, and natural beauty. It boasts over a hundred miles of coastline, making it a beach paradise, and is home to the famous Jersey Shore. Whether you're indulging in a classic taylor ham, egg and cheese sandwich, exploring the state forests, or taking in the stunning views of NYC from Liberty State Park, there's always something exciting to discover in NJ!
  • Total Population: 9.501 million
  • Median Income: $47,833
  • State Bird: American Goldfinch
  • Motto: Liberty and Prosperity
