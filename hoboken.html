Welcome to Hoboken

Craving some delicious food? Fort Lee is home to a diverse and bustling community with excellent dining options. The location also provides easy access to New York City for commuters while still maintaining a suburban feel.

  • Region: Bergen County, Northeastern New Jersey
  • City Classification: suburban/urban
